- Payment Period: From July 27, 2026 to the end of August 09, 2026.

- Students must verify their course registration results and report any issues (if any)
during business hours on August 18–19, 2025.
- After this period, the Office of Academic Affairs will not handle any further
complaints or adjustments related to course registration.
6. Important Notes Regarding Course Registration
To ensure accurate and efficient course registration, students are required to carefully
review and comply with the following regulations and instructions:
- The teaching and exam schedules for Semester 1 of the 2026–2027 academic year for
the English-partial program are developed specifically by cohort (Cohort 12 and 13), major
(Finance and Banking, Accounting, Business Administration, Management Information
Systems, International Economics, English Language), electives by orientation, and campus
(Sai Gon and Thu Duc Campus). Students must thoroughly review these schedules before
registering.
- For elective courses, students must select only those courses aligned with their study
orientation.

- Students are only allowed to register for courses designated for their major.
For example: A Cohort 11 Finance and Banking student must not register for courses
offered to Cohort 11 Accounting or Business Administration students, and vice versa.
- Students must understand their curriculum thoroughly, especially prerequisite
requirements. It is recommended to register for prerequisite courses before other related
courses.
- Students must register for a course load that complies with the minimum and maximum
credit requirements stipulated in the current Academic Regulations, as follows:
+ The minimum course load must be at least two-thirds (2/3) of the average semester
workload specified in the standard study plan.
+ The maximum course load must not exceed one and a half (3/2) times the average
semester workload specified in the standard study plan.
+ For students enrolled in two academic programs simultaneously, the maximum
semester course load limit does not apply.
+ Special courses/modules are not included when calculating the minimum or maximum
course load.
+ To be eligible for scholarship consideration in a regular semester, a student must
register for a minimum course load of 15 credits.
- Students from Cohort K12 onward who have not fulfilled the required entry-level
English language and Information Technology proficiency standards, as stipulated in
Decision No. 894/QĐ-ĐHNH dated April 4, 2025 on Information Technology Competency
Standards and Notice No. 715/TB-ĐHNH dated May 15, 2026 on the Appendix to the
English Language and Information Technology Competency Standards for undergraduate
students at Ho Chi Minh City Banking University, will be subject to semester credit
registration limits in accordance with the University's regulations.
- Students who register fewer than the required minimum or fail to register at all will
receive an academic warning in accordance with university policy.
- Students are advised to keep a printed copy of their registration confirmation in case
any issue arises.
- Students must follow tuition payment announcements from the Finance – Accounting
Department to ensure on-time payment.
+ Students who fail to meet payment deadlines will not be officially enrolled in
registered courses for Semester 1, academic year 2026–2027.
